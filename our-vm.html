VISION

At Svnty Degree, our Our vision for interior design projects is to create transformative spaces that seamlessly merge innovation and sustainability. We believe in crafting environments that are both functional and inspiring, where every element contributes to the well-being and satisfaction of the user. By blending timeless elegance with modern trends, we ensure spaces are both stylish and enduring. Above all, our designs prioritize the needs and comfort of our clients, turning their vision into a reality that exceeds expectations.

Dr. Lal Path Labs

Interior design for Dr. Lal Path Labs focuses on creating clean, functional, and patient-friendly spaces. Key elements include ergonomic layouts, soothing color schemes, and efficient storage solutions to enhance workflow and comfort. The design emphasizes hygiene, professionalism, and trust, reflecting the lab's commitment to quality healthcare.

SBI

SBI Bank's interior design projects focus on creating customer-friendly spaces with modern layouts and ergonomic furniture. The designs prioritize functionality, incorporating digital kiosks, open counters, and dedicated zones for enhanced banking experiences. A clean and professional ambiance reflects the bank's commitment to trust and innovation.

Bank of Baroda

Bank of Baroda's interior design projects focus on creating modern, customer-friendly spaces with an emphasis on functionality and professionalism. The design incorporates sleek furniture, efficient layouts, and vibrant branding elements to reflect trust and innovation. Sustainable materials and technology integration enhance the user experience while maintaining the bank’s global standards.

Bharat Petroleum

The SVNTY Degree Bharat Petroleum Interior Design Project focuses on creating modern and functional spaces that reflect the brand's innovation and sustainability values. The design emphasizes energy-efficient materials, ergonomic layouts, and customer-centric aesthetics. It seamlessly integrates Bharat Petroleum’s identity with futuristic, eco-friendly interior solutions.

Indian Oil Corporation Ltd

Indian Oil Corporation Ltd (IOCL) is India’s largest commercial oil company, involved in refining, pipeline transportation, and marketing of petroleum products. Known for its energy solutions, IOCL also focuses on sustainability and innovation. It operates a vast network of fuel stations and facilities, serving millions across India.

Gurugram University Auditorium

Svnty Degree is a renowned interior design company that designed the Gurugram University Auditorium with a focus on modern aesthetics and functionality. The project features state-of-the-art acoustics, ergonomic seating, and dynamic lighting to create an inspiring space for events. Their expertise blends creativity with technical precision, delivering innovative and impactful designs.
